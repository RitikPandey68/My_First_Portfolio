  • Network activity monitoring to detect suspicious outbound connections
  • USB device monitoring (WMI-based) to identify unauthorized device connections
  • Malware signature scanning with automatic quarantine mechanism
  • Detection of suspicious HTTP traffic and injection attack patterns
